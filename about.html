ABOUT US

Who We Are

The Sidama Education Bureau is the official government body responsible for overseeing education in Sidama Region. We manage over 500 schools across 5 zones, serving more than 15,000 students.

Our Mission

To provide quality education for all students in Sidama Region through accessible registration systems, efficient school management, and continuous improvement of educational services.

Our Vision

To become a model education system in Ethiopia, where every student has access to quality education and seamless registration services.

Our Core Values

  • Accessibility: Education for all, regardless of location or background
  • Transparency: Open and honest registration process
  • Efficiency: Fast and reliable service delivery
  • Innovation: Embracing technology to improve education
  • Community: Working together for Sidama's future
